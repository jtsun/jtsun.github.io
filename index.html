  • Phys. Rev. Lett.

    Floquet band engineering

    The coherent light-matter interaction can make new states of materials under periodic driving. Under the irradiation of circularly polarized light, the black phosphorous (BP) exhibits a variety of Floquet engineered topological states which are inaccessible in equilibrium condition. These topological states in BP manifest the nonequilibrium electron transport and promise for optoelectronic devices.
